1. Overview

This page combines our Privacy Policy, Terms of Use, and Cookie Notice. Gangsu Machinery (“we”) operates a B2B website for plastic recycling equipment. We process inquiry and contact data to provide quotes, engineering proposals, and after-sales support. We do not sell personal information.

2. Privacy Policy

2.1 Information we collect

We collect (1) data you submit via our website forms (name, company, email, phone, material, capacity, message), (2) information you send us via WhatsApp or other channels when you contact us, and (3) basic access and usage data such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited. We do not collect sensitive categories of personal data unless you voluntarily provide them in a message.

2.2 How we use information

We use your data to respond to inquiries, prepare quotes and engineering proposals, communicate about projects and after-sales support, and improve our website and services. We do not use your information for automated decision-making that significantly affects you. We do not sell your personal information.

We do not sell or rent your data. We may share data with service providers that help us operate the website or handle communications (e.g. hosting, email, or form-backend services). Such processors are bound by confidentiality and data-processing obligations. We may disclose data where required by law.

2.5 Data retention

We retain inquiry and contact data for as long as needed to fulfil the purpose (e.g. ongoing projects and support) and to comply with legal obligations. You may ask us to delete or restrict your data subject to applicable law.

2.6 Your choices

You can choose not to submit forms or not to contact us via WhatsApp. You can use browser settings to limit cookies. In some jurisdictions you have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your data or to object to processing; contact us to exercise them.

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. No system is completely secure; we do not guarantee absolute security.
