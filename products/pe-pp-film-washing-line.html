Plastic Agricultural Film Greenhouse Film Washing Line

Our plastic agricultural film washing line is specifically designed for cleaning heavily contaminated post-agricultural PE films, greenhouse films, and mulch films. The complete system handles all critical stages from shredding to final drying, producing clean flakes ready for pelletizing.

View details
Capacity range: 300–2000 kg/h for different scale operations
Advanced friction washers and hot wash tanks for deep cleaning
Multi-stage dewatering system for ≤5% moisture content
Optimized water recycling to minimize consumption
