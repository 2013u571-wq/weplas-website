Specialized shredding system for long-length plastics, pipes and profiles. Our pipe shredder handles PVC, PE, PP pipes, window profiles and extrusion scrap with efficient in-feed and cutting design for continuous recycling operations.

Product Overview

The pipe and profile shredder is built for long-length plastic waste. Specialized feeding and cutting mechanisms process pipes, profiles and extrusion scrap into uniform flakes. Applications include PVC pipes, PE/PP pipes, window profiles, door frames and extrusion offcuts.

Gangsu pipe shredders are designed for 24/7 operation with robust construction, easy maintenance and integration with downstream washing or pelletizing lines. Options include conveyor feeding, different cutting chamber sizes and custom screen configurations.

FAQ

What is a pipe and profile shredder used for?

Pipe shredders are specialized for long-length plastic waste: PVC pipes, PE/PP pipes, window profiles, door frames and extrusion scrap. They cut and shred these materials into uniform flakes for washing, granulating or pelletizing.

What pipe lengths can the shredder handle?

Depending on the model, we can process pipes and profiles from a few hundred mm up to 6 meters or more. Contact us for your specific material and length requirements.

Can it integrate with a complete recycling line?

Yes. The pipe shredder is often the first stage before washing and pelletizing. We can design the complete line including feeding, shredding, washing and granulation.
