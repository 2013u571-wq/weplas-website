Waste Rigid Plastic Washing Line

Our rigid plastic washing line is specifically designed for cleaning heavily contaminated HDPE containers, oil pots, buckets, crates and drums. The complete system handles all critical stages from shredding to final drying, producing clean flakes ready for pelletizing or direct use.

Capacity range: 300–2000 kg/h for different scale operations
Advanced friction washers and hot wash tanks for deep cleaning
Effective oil and grease removal system
Output purity ≥98% for high-quality recycling
