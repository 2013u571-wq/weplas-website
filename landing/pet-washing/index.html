Process Flow (Typical)

Final configuration depends on your contamination level and target application. Below is a common high-efficiency process for clear/light-blue bottles.

1
Bale Opening & Pre-Sorting

Remove metals/large contaminants. Improve stability for the whole line.

2
Label Removal & Pre-Wash

Dry friction or steam / water pre-wash to reduce adhesive & dirt load.

3
Crushing + Washing

Wet crushing reduces dust; transfers flakes into washing stage smoothly.

4
Hot Wash + Rinsing

Remove oils/adhesives. Multi-stage rinsing improves purity.

5
Float-Sink Separation

Separate PP/PE caps/labels from PET by density. Key for high purity.

6
Dewatering & Drying

Mechanical + thermal drying to reach low moisture and stable bagging.

FAQ (Pre-Answers = More Leads)

Can this line handle bottles with caps and labels?

Yes. We typically use label remover + float-sink separation to remove PP/PE caps/labels and improve purity.

Do I need hot washing?

If your bottles have oils/adhesives/organics or you target higher purity, hot washing is strongly recommended.

What info do you need for a quotation?

Material type, contamination level, required capacity, end-use (fiber/sheet/strap/B2B), factory power/water limits.
